What Are Face Shapes?

Face shape is determined by the relative widths and lengths of key facial landmarks: the forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and overall face length. Anthropometry—the measurement of human facial dimensions—has roots in early 20th-century studies, seeking to quantify beauty and health5. Historically, the “golden ratio” (≈1.618) was applied to facial proportions, but its practical use has largely given way to precise linear measurements and modern imaging techniques6.

Methods to Determine Face Shape (Manual vs AI)

Manual Anthropometric Techniques

Traditional methods use a soft tape measure or calipers to record:

  • Face length: hairline to chin
  • Forehead width: midway between hairline & eyebrows
  • Cheekbone width: outer eye corner to corner
  • Jawline width: jaw angle to angle

Comparing these values via facial index formulas or golden-ratio checks provides a manual classification8.

AI-Driven Analysis

AI face-shape detectors use computer vision to locate landmarks automatically, measure distances, and match proportions against predefined models. This yields rapid, objective classification and can even handle batch processing of images9. Such tools reduce human error and processing time from minutes to seconds9.

Styling & Cosmetic Recommendations by Face Shape

Choosing the right hairstyle, makeup, and accessories can accentuate one’s best features. Below are expert-backed tips for each shape:

Oval

Nearly any style works. Add volume at temples or soft waves to balance proportions13.

Round

Create height at the crown, keep sides sleek, and avoid chin-length curls that widen the face14.

Square

Soften angles with layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and tousled waves15.

Heart

Use side-parted bangs or chin-skimming bobs to minimize forehead width16.

Diamond

Emphasize cheekbones with textured layers and fringe to balance a narrow forehead17.

Oblong

Shorten appearance with blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, or fullness at the sides14.

Triangle

Add volume at temples, use highlights around forehead, and taper hairstyles at the jaw16.
